The Worst Seat in the World

Contemporary Romance Ongoing Wattpad

Synopsis

Maya Reyes has a system for everything, including long-haul flights. Snacks in order. Window seat secured. Fourteen hours mapped out.

Then 22C sits down.

Daniel Park is an architect, he smells like cedar soap, and he has just taken the armrest without a single word of negotiation. He is also the most straightforward person Maya has talked to in years. They land at JFK and she expects to never see him again.

He is standing near the arrivals doors.

The Worst Seat in the World is a slow-burn romance about two people who meet in the worst possible seat and spend the rest of the story realizing it was the best possible seat.
