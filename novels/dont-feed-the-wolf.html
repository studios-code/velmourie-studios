Don't Feed the Wolf

Romance Completed Inkitt

Synopsis

Nora Vass has been watching Fen Calloway for four months.

He has been watching her back.

She's a journalist. He runs a network the city pretends doesn't exist. She needs what he knows. He needs what only she can do with it. Neither of them planned on the part that comes after.

Don't Feed the Wolf is a slow-burn romance about two people who are both, in their own way, very good at their jobs. And very bad at what comes next.
