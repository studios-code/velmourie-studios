The Worst Seat in the World

Contemporary Romance · Completed

Maya Reyes has a system for everything, including long-haul flights. Snacks in order. Window seat secured. Fourteen hours mapped out. Then 22C sits down. Daniel Park is an architect, he smells like cedar soap, and he has just taken the armrest without a single word of negotiation. He is also the most straightforward person Maya has talked to in years. They land at JFK and she expects to never see him again. He is standing near the arrivals doors. The Worst Seat in the World is a slow-burn romance about two people who meet in the worst possible seat and spend the rest of the story realizing it was the best possible seat.

Don't Feed the Wolf

Romance · Completed

Nora Vass has been watching Fen Calloway for four months. He has been watching her back. She's a journalist. He runs a network the city pretends doesn't exist. She needs what he knows. He needs what only she can do with it. Neither of them planned on the part that comes after. Don't Feed the Wolf is a slow-burn romance about two people who are both, in their own way, very good at their jobs. And very bad at what comes next.

Six Feet of Bad Attitude

Sports Romance · Ongoing

Soren Vask transferred with no explanation and a reputation for being impossible. Rue Callisto covers the team. She never chases the interview. She just shows up every morning and pays attention. He notices. Six Feet of Bad Attitude is a romance about a man who only feels like himself on the pitch, and a woman who makes him feel like himself everywhere else.

悪役令嬢に転生したので、氷公爵の「不要な花嫁」になりました

Fantasy Romance · Completed

ブラック企業で過労死した私は、乙女ゲームの悪役令嬢リリアナに転生していた。待っていたのは破滅エンドのはずが、なぜか“氷公爵”シュトラウスの婚約者に選ばれてしまう。感情のない契約関係のはずだったのに、静かな日々の中で少しずつ距離が変わっていく。これは、不要なはずの花嫁が見つける、穏やかな恋の物語。
